About TOMODACHI Coffee

Welcome to TOMODACHI Coffee where friendship begins with every cup.
Our name “Tomodachi” means “friend” in Japanese, and that’s exactly
what we want every guest to feel like when they walk through our doors
a friend, not just a customer.

At TOMODACHI Coffee, our vision is to create a place where
coffee and friendship come together in harmony. Our mission is
to serve the finest quality coffee made from ethically sourced beans,
prepared with care, consistency, and passion.
